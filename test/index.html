How will Picklehack be held? ▼

Virtually! Join us from your bedroom, dorm room, or whatever funky zoom background you want to use.

We're looking for your silliest, goofiest, and most useless hacks for a light hearted break from our current environment. (Almost) anything's fair game!

What if I don't have a team? ▼

Those looking for a team can fill out the specific questions on the application, which will be used in our team-making script. You will be notified of your team grouping soon after applications close.
